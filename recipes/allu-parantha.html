Allu Parantha

Description

Allu Parantha is very popular indian dish in the we stuff dow with patato mixure

Ingredients

  • flour
  • Butter
  • patatos
  • spices
  • onion

Steps

  1. Boil the patatos and mess them
  2. Add spices and onion
  3. make ball of dow and put the mess patato mixure into the dow
  4. Make it flate
  5. tabe per sekho achche se butter laga kr.
