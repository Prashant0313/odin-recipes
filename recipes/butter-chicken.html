Butter chicken

Description

a classic Indian curry dish consisting of marinated chicken pieces simmered in a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced tomato and butter-based gravy

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • Butter
  • oil
  • spices
  • tomato

Steps

  1. Manirate the chicken into the base of yogurt and some spices
  2. Take paste of onion and tomato fried it
  3. After some time when our onion and tomato paste get rosted add some spices of your choice
  4. Add chicken into the paste
  5. Add butter
